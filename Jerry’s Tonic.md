# Jerry’s Tonic 

*Sailor Jerry spiced rum with iced tea, tonic and bitters*

## Ingredients

| Ingredient                  | Amount      |
|-----------------------------|-------------|
| Sailor Jerry Spiced Rum     | 1 part      |
| Lipton Peach Iced Tea (tin) | 2 parts     |
| Tonic water                 | 1.5 parts   |
| Simple syrup                | 0.5 parts   |
| Angostura bitters           | 2 dashes    |
| Vanilla essence             | 1 dash      |
| Lime wedge                  | 1, squeezed |

---

## Method

1. Chill a tin of Lipton Peach Iced Tea and tonic water
2. Add rum, peach iced tea, simple syrup, vanilla essence, bitters and lime juice to a shaker with ice
3. Shake well
4. Strain into a tall glass over fresh ice
5. Top with chilled tonic water — pour gently down the side to keep the fizz
6. Garnish with a lime wedge

---

## Notes

- Go easy on the vanilla essence, it's potent — a small dash is enough
- Use a good quality chilled tonic — Fever-Tree or similar
