# Recipes

This is my personal recipe vault. I do not claim any of the content here as original work; these recipes are collected from various sources across the internet, cookbooks, and other materials.  These are the recipe I ignore when I cook but usually form the basis of what I actually ignore.

I do a lot of charcoal barbecuing on either a kettle or a kamado. I also use a sous vide cooker a great deal and find rice cookers hard to resist. My view is that five-ply copper pans and a good extractor fan are as important as a sharp knife. My favourite is the Zwilling J.A. Henckels Twin Master Chef's Knife, and a good knife sharpener.
